**Different Units of Ether:** 

-  Wei           =>  1 Wei 
-  Babbage  =>  10 ** 3 Wei 
-  Lovelace  =>  10 ** 6 Wei 
-  Shannon  =>  10 ** 9 Wei 
-  Szabo      =>  10 ** 12 Wei 
-  Finney     =>  10 ** 15 Wei 
-  Ether       =>  10 ** 18 Wei 

- Gwei (Shannon): Used for gas fees in Etheruem transactions.
- Wei: The smallest and most fundamental unit.
